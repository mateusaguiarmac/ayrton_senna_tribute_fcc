Ayrton Senna

The Legendary Racer

Here's a timeline of Ayrton Senna's life:

  • 1960 - Born in Sao Paulo, Brazil
  • 1973 - Started his career competing in kart
  • 1981 - Started racing "formula cars", when he won Ford Formulas 1600 and 2000.
  • 1983 - Won the Britain's Formula 3 championship, beating lots of records.
  • 1984 - His first race in Formula 1, at the Brazilian Grand Prix. His first team was Toleman-Hart.
  • 1985 - Left Toleman-Hart and joined Lotus-Renault.
  • 1988 - Joined Alain Prost in McLaren-Honda team. Senna won 8 races that year and became the world champion for the first time.
  • 1990 - Won his second Fomula 1 championship.
  • 1991 - Won his third world championship. Became the youngest three times Formula 1 champion.
  • 1994 - Joined Williams team, racing only three times, the last one being the San Marino Grand Prix, where he was involved in an accident in which he end up dying.

"I have no idols. I have admiration for work, dedication and competence." - Ayrton Senna
